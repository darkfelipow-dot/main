Summary
NPC Mayomayo in Malangdo offers weapon enchants for Level 3–4 weapons using Malangdo coins. Coin class determines available enchant types. Enchants are guaranteed (no fail) and can be reset.

Overview

In Malangdo, there is an NPC Mayomayo (navi: (malangdo 213/167)) who can add enchantments to many level 3 and 4 weapons in exchange for Malangdo coins. Machines near Mayomayo convert coin types and allow conversion of Silvervine Fruit to A Class Coins. Enchants use a Cost Multiplier for each weapon; the coins required are Base Cost × Multiplier.

  • Enchants can be reset with 1 Silvervine Fruit (resets all enchants).
  • There is no chance of failure — an enchant is always given.
  • Cards and refines are not lost.
  • You must have the weapon equipped to enchant or reset.
  • A Class / Seagod's Anger allow selecting Melee / Ranged / Magic types.
  • Weapons with 3 card slots can only receive one Malangdo enchant.

Friday es una instancia que sólo se puede entrar los viernes. Dentro, los jugadores pueden farmear Crafted Stone (account-bound) y material para enchants, y existe la posibilidad de invocar al MVP Lich Lord.

Overview

Friday is an instance with level-locked monster spawns and special rewards: crafted stones for enchants and rare drops such as the Royal Guardian Ring. The page preserves item images pointing to Divine‑Pride (static) as the canonical image source.

Guide

  1. Enter Geffen Tower and talk to Mariage to create/enter the instance.
    (geffen 119/112)
  2. Inside talk to Mariage and select desired spawn levels (100, 160, etc.).
  3. Farm monsters and look for treasure spawns / special spawn mechanics to obtain Crafted Stones and attempt MVP summons.
